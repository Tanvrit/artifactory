## Logo Usage Guidelines

### The Tanvrit Mark
The 5-circle constellation T-mark represents Tanvrit's 5 product verticals unified
under one platform. It should never be recolored, distorted, or have effects applied.

### Minimum Sizes
| Platform | Minimum Size |
|----------|-------------|
| Print    | 10mm / 28px |
| Digital  | 32×32px     |
| Favicon  | 16×16px     |

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space equal to the diameter of one circle in the mark on all sides.

### Color Variants
- **Primary (dark bg):** Forest Green mark on dark background
- **Reversed (light bg):** Forest Green mark on white/light background
- **Monochrome:** Single-color black or white mark

### Do NOT
- Recolor the mark
- Add drop shadows, bevels, or gradients not in the original
- Stretch or skew
- Place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Use the T letterform alone without the circle constellation
